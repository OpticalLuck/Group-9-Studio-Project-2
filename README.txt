-------------------- USER GUIDE ----------------------

Objective:
Complete Quests
 - Visit Mayor
 - Find Gem in City (Check in between Buildings)
 - Go Stadium And Go through 16 Rings without falling to the ground and collect gem in middle afterwards
 - Visit and talk to librarian, collect gem
 - Give Gem to mayor and grab Key


==================== CONTROLS =====================

Mouse Panning - Move Camera
WASD - Movement
Space - Jump / Glide (While Falling)
Shift - Sprint
Left Click - Talk to NPCs
E - Interact
Tab - Quest List
Escape - Pause
